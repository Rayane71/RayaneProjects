Robin Williams's Death

Robin Williams deserved better

After Suffering from Lewy Body Dementia, that wasn't diagnosed until after his death, and suffering from parkinson's Disease, Robin hanged himself with a belt and left us on August 11th 2014

Robin was losing his mind and he was aware of it.
Can you imagine the pain he felt as he experienced himself disintegrating?

Williams not only amplified and brought awareness to Lewy Body Dementia, but his passing brought much needed attention to the importance of mental health and the symptoms of depression and anxiety that can accompany a neurodegenerative disease.
